# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Farmers** (primary, mobile-first): small/medium growers and FPO groups, often rural, varied literacy and connectivity. Used standing in a field or home on a phone, frequently low-bandwidth. Job: list produce, see a fair live price, reach many buyers, close a deal and get paid safely without a middleman skimming margin.

**Dealers** (local-market, exporter, company-supplier): discover verified farmers, negotiate, order, pay, arrange logistics. More data-dense, desktop + mobile, often managing many lots and buy-requirements.

**Admin / back-office**: KYC verification (PAN/GST), moderation, dispute mediation, master data.

## Product Purpose

A digital marketplace taking a vegetable/fruit trade through its full lifecycle — discover → negotiate → order → pay → deliver → rate — connecting farmers and dealers directly. It removes intermediaries, exposes live mandi prices (eNAM/Agmarknet), and enforces trust via verified KYC profiles, completed-order-only reviews, escrow-style payment holds, and dispute resolution. Success = a farmer reaches more buyers and is paid fairly and safely; a dealer reliably sources fresh, verified produce.

## Brand Personality

**Trustworthy, grounded, fair.** Reliability is the emotional core: real money, bulk volumes, rural-first users who must believe the platform protects them. Voice is plain, direct, and respectful — never clever at the user's expense. Confidence without slickness; the design should feel like infrastructure you can lean on, not a startup pitch.

## Anti-references

- **Cream / beige / parchment AI-default look** — no warm-neutral near-white bg, eyebrow kickers, numbered section scaffolding, or identical icon-card grids.
- **Cluttered banner-heavy Indian e-commerce** — no loud-discount popups, dense promo clutter, screaming reds.
- **Cold corporate fintech** (navy + gold banking sterility) — users are rural-first; warmth and clarity over institutional stiffness.
- **Toy-like / cartoonish** — handles real bulk transactions; must stay credible.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust is visible.** Verification, escrow state, order status, and price provenance are surfaced plainly — never buried. The user always knows where their money and produce stand.
2. **Legible under stress.** Designed for sunsteady connections, sunlight, low literacy, and small/old devices. High contrast, large tap targets, plain language, voice and large-icon affordances are first-class, not add-ons.
3. **Price honesty.** Live market price is context the farmer sees before they set their own — fairness through transparency, not persuasion.
4. **Workflow over decoration.** Every screen serves the next step in discover→pay→deliver. Reduce taps; surface the one primary action.
5. **Grounded, not loud.** Calm and credible beats flashy; the produce and the deal are the subject, the UI gets out of the way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast (critical for outdoor/sunlight use). Multi-language UI. Voice input + read-aloud and a simplified large-icon mode for low/non-literacy users. Large tap targets (≥44px). Offline / low-bandwidth tolerance (cached listings, queued actions). Reduced-motion alternative for all motion. Don't rely on color alone for order/dispute/verification state.
